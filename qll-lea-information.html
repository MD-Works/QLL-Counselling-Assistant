What you have

A chat assistant that knows your practice inside out

Lea is an AI assistant that lives on your website as a small chat bubble. When a visitor clicks it, they can ask questions about your services, pricing, booking, and how you work — and get accurate, warm answers, day or night, without you having to be available.

Unlike a generic chatbot, Lea only knows what you've told her. Every answer she gives comes directly from your knowledge base — a document we've built together that contains your services, your values, your pricing, and your own words. She cannot make things up, and she will never tell a client something you haven't approved.

Lea is completely free to run. There are no monthly subscriptions, no per-message fees, and no hidden costs. She uses a free AI service called Groq, and is hosted on Cloudflare — both of which offer generous free tiers that comfortably cover a practice like QLL.

Built-in safety

Guardrails that protect your clients and your practice

A mental health and wellness practice requires more care than an ordinary chatbot. Lea has strict safety rules that cannot be turned off.

🛡️ Always active, always protected
✓ Crisis detection. If a visitor uses keywords associated with suicidal thoughts, self-harm, abuse, or immediate danger — Lea immediately stops and displays the SADAG helpline, the Suicide Crisis Line, Lifeline, and the GBV Command Centre. This happens before any AI response is generated. No delay, no exceptions.
✓ No diagnoses, ever. If someone asks "do I have depression?" or "am I bipolar?" — Lea declines clearly, explains this is outside what she can help with, and suggests contacting you for a proper conversation.
✓ Stays in her lane. Lea only answers using your approved knowledge base. She cannot invent prices, invent services, or make promises on your behalf. If she doesn't know the answer, she says so and directs the client to contact you.
✓ Clear identity. Lea always identifies herself as an information assistant — not a counsellor, not Elizabeth. The chat panel displays "General information only · Not a counsellor" at all times.
✓ No medical advice. Lea is instructed never to give medical advice, suggest treatments outside your scope, or contradict your professional guidance.
How it works

Simple for visitors, invisible for you

When someone asks Lea a question, here is what happens in the background — in plain English.

1
A visitor types a question
For example: "How much does a Reiki session cost?" or "Can I book online?" The question is first checked against a list of crisis and safety keywords before anything else happens.
2
Lea searches your knowledge base
She looks for the entries in your knowledge base that are most relevant to the question — matching keywords and phrases to find the best factual source. This is the "retrieval" part of the system.
3
She composes a natural response
Using only the matched entries as her source, the AI writes a warm, natural reply — in the tone and personality you've chosen. She does not use any information from outside your knowledge base.
4
The visitor gets a helpful answer
The response appears in the chat in seconds. If the visitor has a follow-up question, Lea remembers the conversation context and responds accordingly — just like a real conversation.

The tool

The Widget Wizard — what Morney uses to configure Lea

The Widget Wizard is the tool used to build, customise, and export Lea for your website. It works in four steps.

Step 1 · Identity
Set the bot's name (currently "Lea"), connect the Groq AI service, and point to your knowledge base. This is done once and rarely needs changing.
Step 2 · Personality
Choose the tone Lea uses when responding. Four options are available: Warm & Nurturing (gentle, empathetic — currently selected), Calm & Professional, Bright & Approachable, or Grounded & Wise. Each maps to a different set of instructions given to the AI. You can also add a custom instruction — for example, "always mention the free discovery call when relevant."
Step 3 · Appearance
Set the colours, avatar emoji, and whether the chat bubble sits in the bottom-right or bottom-left corner. A live preview updates as you make changes, so you see exactly how Lea will look on your site before exporting.
Step 4 · Export
The wizard produces two files: a standalone HTML page for testing, and a script snippet your website developer can paste into any page with a single line of code. No complex integration required.
